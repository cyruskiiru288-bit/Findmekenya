Need Help?

Here are answers to common questions:

How do I register as a fundi?

Click "Offer Your Services" on the homepage, fill in your details and choose a subscription plan.

How much does it cost?

Ksh 32/month, Ksh 80 for 3 months or Ksh 180 for 6 months. First 500 fundis get 6 months FREE!

How do clients contact me?

Clients find your profile and contact you directly via Call, WhatsApp or Facebook.

What happens if my subscription expires?

Your profile will be hidden from search results until you renew your subscription via M-Pesa.
